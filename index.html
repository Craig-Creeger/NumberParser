You may enter a percent symbol in the number field to have it evaluated as a percent.

The contents of a textbox are always a string, but if you want it evaluated as a number, click the Number radio button (you will need to enter a valid number in the textbox).

Due to the way computers store floating point numbers, you may see an unexpected number in the toPercent field. You can round the number by entering a numeric value in the Decimal Places box.
